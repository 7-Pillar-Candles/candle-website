Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do you take custom orders?

Yes! I love creating custom candles for special occasions like weddings, birthdays, or corporate events. Just reach out via WhatsApp or email with your ideas, and we'll work together to create something perfect for you.

How long do the candles burn?

Burn time varies by size, but on average: small candles (4-6 hours), medium candles (15-20 hours), and large pillar candles (30-40 hours). Proper care like trimming the wick extends burn time.

What materials do you use?

All candles are made with premium soy wax, natural essential oils, and eco-friendly materials. They're non-toxic, burn cleanly, and are better for you and the environment than paraffin candles.

Do you offer shipping?

Yes! I ship across India. Shipping costs vary by location and order size. Local pickup in Bangalore is also available. Contact me for exact shipping quotes.

How should I care for my candle?

Trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn. Burn for 2-3 hours at a time to create an even wax pool. Keep away from drafts and never leave burning candles unattended.

Can I choose specific scents or colors?

Absolutely! For custom orders, you can choose from a variety of scents and colors. I can also create scent combinations tailored to your preferences.

Our Story

Handcrafted with care from our home studio

Made with Love

Hi, I'm Mary Poulin! I create these candles by hand from my home studio in Bangalore. Each candle is a blend of natural soy wax, fine fragrances, and a whole lot of heart.

What started as a creative hobby has blossomed into my passion. I love experimenting with unique designs like bubble towers, cosmic pillars, and decorative tins. Each piece is crafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality.

The name "7 Pillar" represents the seven pillars of wellness: physical, emotional, social, intellectual, spiritual, environmental, and occupational. My hope is that each candle brings light, warmth, and positive energy into your life.
